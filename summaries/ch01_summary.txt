**Chapter 1: Turning Point**

In the dimly lit receiving room of a secure facility, Mara Quill receives contradictory evidence about her sister’s involvement in a data breach threatening a highly secretive financial institution. Ivo, her colleague and ally, reveals a risky lead that could potentially close one path but opens a costlier one. Mara must make a crucial decision, which she does with guarded confidence. The scene ends on this irreversible choice, leaving the audience to wonder about the new challenges ahead for Mara and her sister.

**Unresolved Threads:**
- The true extent of the data breach remains unclear.
- Mara’s conflicting emotions and calculated risks are highlighted but unresolved.
- The full implications of Ivo’s risky lead are not yet known.
